About.

Velvet Hammer has created a modern-day standard for independent music companies. This innovative and dedicated outfit handles all of the multifaceted components of its clients’ careers, including A&R, production, marketing, branding, touring, etc...

Velvet Hammer strives for continued, long-term success – an approach that embodies the “strength in elasticity,” rather than the “quick fix” philosophy. The company’s focus is premised on worldwide, timeless brand-relevance, rather than just radio and record cycle presence.

The company’s management roster is home to Grammy-winning bands that include AFI, System Of A Down, Deftones, Alice In Chains, Korn, The Smashing Pumpkins, Code Orange, Night Riots, Crosses, Scars On Broadway, Blaqk Audio and XTRMST.

In addition to artist management, Velvet Hammer has also signed and released records for several artists that include OneRepublic, Poison The Well and others.
