About Wholesale Barstool Club

After several years in the retail furniture industry, we realized that there is a huge demand and inadequate supply of places to get reasonable deals on commercial and residential bar stools, restaurant tables and chairs, affordable office furniture.

Traditional price mark ups were limiting what our residential and commercial clients could afford for their home or business! Wholesale Barstool Club is here to bridge that gap and get top quality bar stools, restaurant furniture, and more at wholesale pricing!

We can ship in stock products usually
within 48 hours anywhere you need

the equipment to be!

All of this adds up to keeping money in your pocket while getting exactly what you need!
