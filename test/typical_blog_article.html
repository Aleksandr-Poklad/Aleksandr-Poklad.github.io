10 UNIQUE WHOLESALE STOOLS
TO SPRUCE UP YOUR RESTAURANT OR HOME

The slightest addition of some color can change the whole attitude of a room. A splash of red in a grey room will bring it to life. The subtle smell of leather at the bar can create a cool, sexy atmosphere. And a fun office chair can change your whole approach to going into your office.

Here at Wholesale Barstool Club, we offer a wide variety of bar stools. Some are plain and some are wild, some are spicy and some are mild. But thing’s for sure, we have what you’re looking for. Here are ten unique wholesale stools to spruce up your restaurant or home.

1) The Backless Saddle Stool

Style AND comfort coming standard with the black backless saddle stool.

2) The Stackable Stool with Orange Seat and Silver Powder Coated Frame

Perfect for the classroom and/or the house, this smaller stool is great in many places.

3) Vibrant Candy Heart Tractor Seat and Chrome Stool

Not only are backless stools good for posture, but these are colorful and fun!

4) 29'' Backless Cherry Wood Bar Stool With Black Leather Swivel Seat

Nailhead trim, a swivel top, and cherry wood. What else could you want in a bar stool?

5) 24” Backless Black Metal Counter Height Stool

A fantastic value, these are some of Wholesale Bar Stool Club's best sellers for multiple reasons: price, style, reliability, and variety of color.

6) Contemporary Blue Plastic Adjustable Height Bar Stool With Chrome Base

You want modern? We have modern. Just check out this adjustable height wholesale stool with a chrome base in multiple colors.

7) Contemporary Red Plastic Adjustable Height Bar Stool With Chrome Base

With a chrome base, a foot rest, and a plethora of colors, this bar stools is as unique as they come.

8) Beech Bentwood Adjustable Height Bar Stool With Black Vinyl Upholstery

The beech bentwood is a perfect addition to any modern apartment, flat, or home that needs a counter-height addition.

9) Contemporary Brown Vinyl Adjustable Height Bar Stool With Chrome Base

If you prefer a back to your bar stool, check out this contemporary brown adjustable wholesale stool.

10) Contemporary White Vinyl Adjustable Height Bar Stool with Arms and Chrome Base

*** BONUS STOOL ***

11) 30” Silver Metal Bar Stool

As an added bonus, check out this sturdy stool with a back.
